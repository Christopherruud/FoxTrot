Quiz Editor

Create your quiz!

Write as many questions as you like. When you are finished with a question click Next to create a new question

or Done to finish the quiz.

You can choose from the different types of answers you wish from the menu on the right side of the question field.

Describe your question carefully. The quiz-takers should be able to answer by multiple choice or by true/false

To create a new course: write a name in the textfield and click "Create"
